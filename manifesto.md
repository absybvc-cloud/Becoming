# Becoming Manifesto

Music was once an object.

A song.
A recording.
An album.

But time itself never stops.

Becoming asks a simple question:

What happens if music is not recorded,
but released into time?

In Becoming the album is no longer a container.
The album is a system.

The system generates a sonic world.

Sounds appear, interact, dissolve,
and return in new forms.

Each listener enters a different moment.

No one hears the whole album.

Becoming is not composed once.

It continuously becomes itself.

Music no longer ends.

Music continues to exist.
